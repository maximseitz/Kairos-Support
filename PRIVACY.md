# Privacy Policy for Kairos

**Last Updated: May 8, 2026**

Kairos is built with a "Sovereign-by-Design" philosophy. We believe that your time and your data belong exclusively to you. 

### 1. No Data Collection
Kairos does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or telemetry. 

### 2. Local Storage Only
All data created within Kairos—including time blocks, logs, notes, and task descriptions—is stored **exclusively on your local device**. 
* The developer has no access to your data.
* No third-party analytics or tracking services are integrated into the app.

### 3. Data Control and Sovereignty
Since all data is stored locally, you are in full control:
* Your data never leaves your Mac unless you manually export it.
* Deleting the app or its local database permanently removes all associated data from your device.

### 4. Third-Party Services
Kairos does not use any third-party services, APIs, or cloud providers that could access your data.

### 5. Compliance with Apple’s Privacy Requirements
For the purposes of the App Store, Kairos is classified as an app that **does not collect any user data**.
